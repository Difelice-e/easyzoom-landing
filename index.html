FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about Easyzoom within our FAQ.

  • What is Easyzoom?

    Easyzoom is an NFT analysis tool that provides advanced analytics and visualization capabilities, allowing users to easily understand and interpret the data. As a member of our DAO, you'll have a direct say in which features we add and how our tool evolves, also you'll be rewarded for every future subscription to the tool and NFTs flips.

  • What are the requirements to join the Easyzoom DAO?

    To join our DAO, you'll need to purchase an Easyzoom pass, which is a lifetime NFT that grants you access to the DAO and its benefits. Additionally, you'll need to stake your NFT to ensure proper protection and valorization of the NFTs.

  • Are the founds of the DAO transparent?

    Yes, the funds of the DAO will always be visible on the dashboard, providing complete transparency and oversight at all times.

  • What is the community wallet and how does it work?

    The community wallet is a shared wallet managed by the DAO which receives funds from different sources like NFT minting, royalties, subscription service revenue and NFT flipping. These funds are then used to purchase NFTs and perform other operations as decided by the members of the DAO.

  • Can i sell my Easyzoom pass after purchasing it?

    Yes, you can sell your Easyzoom pass at any time, but keep in mind that if you do, you'll lose the ability to claim the profit and it will be absorbed by the treasury. Additionally, the new holder will only be able to redeem the dividend for its holding period. The whole thing is based on a staking mechanism.

  • How often can i claim my share of the profits?

    The staking system that will be based on a quarterly or biannual basis. This will allow for regular rewards to be distributed to members. However, to do so, you’ll need to stake your NFT, this way you will ensure a proper protection and valorization of the NFTs.
