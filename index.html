What is Easyzoom?

At Easyzoom, we believe in giving our users the tools they need to shape the future. We already provide you wih a robust and high-performing tool foundation and now, as a member of our DAO council you'll have a direct say in which features we add and how our tool evolves.

By minting your own Easyzoom pass, you'll not only have a role in the evolution of the tool but also be rewarded for every future subscription to the tool enjoying a tangible reward.
